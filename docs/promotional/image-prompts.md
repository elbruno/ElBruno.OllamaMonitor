## 10. Design Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary Green: #22C55E (success / running)
- Primary Blue: #3B82F6 (model loaded)
- Orange: #F97316 (high usage)
- Gray: #6B7280 (offline)
- Red: #EF4444 (error)
- Dark Background: #1F2937
- Light Text: #F3F4F6

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Segoe UI, Roboto)
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Code/Monospace: Consolas, Monaco, or similar

**Style:**
- Flat design, minimal shadows
- Geometric, clean shapes
- Lots of whitespace
- Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
- Icons should be recognizable at small sizes (16×16 tray icon)

**Do Not Include:**
- Too much text
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Outdated design aesthetics
- Conflicting color schemes
- Illegible fonts at small sizes
